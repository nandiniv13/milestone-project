About Me

I am based out of Newark ,Delaware and live with my small family. I started my career as an Lighting Application Specialist. Helping large corporations and small business owners to design lighting solutions. But my true love has always been art.

Started upgrading my tech skills to build my own personal website for my artwork. Visual Design skills I learnt helped me bridge the gap between tech and art.

Now with my skills in web design & development , I am a full-time consultant for your business needs from planning to execution.

Skills
